Joy'N'Ride

joyNride is a free crowd-sourcing service which allows you share future voyages by car
and save money while sharing the car with fellow travellers.
The payment is equally spent among all participants, and is set by the driver itself,
as well as the source and the destination of the voyage.

Search results are filtered according to parameters of price, source, destination and whether
or not the transaction has filled up with passengers. The source of a displayed transaction has to
be located near the supplied source within a radius specified by the driver,
whereas the destination itself has to be located near some selected points along the transaction path,
within a radius specified by the driver.
joyNride encourages users to rate the transaction after taking part in it, and even rewards users which tend
to rate by presenting multiple requests to a driver (by different users) in a specific order. to be continued...
